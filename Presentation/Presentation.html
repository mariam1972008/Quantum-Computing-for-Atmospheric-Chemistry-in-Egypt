From Shaaban's Story to a National Problem

"Cough... cough"... this is not just a cough, but an echo of an environmental and health problem that affects the lives of thousands of Egyptians along the banks of the Nile. It's a story that starts with the cough of "Uncle Shaaban," a simple farmer, and extends to reveal the erosion of his home, raising a troubling question: what is the hidden connection between the Nile, our health, and the future of our infrastructure?
